Plans and Pricing

So what are you buying with Attachmore anyway?

Attachmore pricing plans are based on how much data you upload a month. You have unlimited monthly upload sessions. Every 30 days the upload limit is reset and you can upload to your limit again.
Attachmore is different than Online Storage offerings which base pricing on how much is stored. Online Storage sites are designed as archival or collaboration sites. These are great tools for backup or multi-team collaborations. but are often too overwhelming and complicated for the simple task of file sharing. Attachmore is a file transfer service thus our pricing is based on how much you transfer instead of how much you store.

The Value of Attachmore

The value of using Attachmore is simplicity. With Attachmore. you upload. receive a link and share the link. Your recipients use the link to download your files and that's it. The file sharing goal was accomplished. The download link automatically expires and your files automatically purge from Attachmore. You are not bothered with managing user access. or an online archive of your files. It is simple peace of mind from simple file sharing.
