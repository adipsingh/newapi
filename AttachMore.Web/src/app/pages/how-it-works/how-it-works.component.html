How it works

Uploading Large Files with ease

1

Upload to Attachmore

Select Files or Folders Using the Attachmore Website or the Attachmore Upload Software.
With the website you select files to upload using a file explorer that displays the files and folders on your computer.
With the Attachmore Software. you select files and folders by right clicking them and selecting "Upload to Attachmore" from the shortcut menu.

3

Send and Share the Download Link

Attachmore connects with all your favorite communication tools to help you send and share download links easily. Select one of the many options and Attachmore opens a compose message. The download link automatically inserts into the body of the message and your are ready to compose and send your message. If we don't list your communication tool. you can use the Copy Link button to copy the link to your computer's clipboard. Then you can paste the link anywhere you want.

2

Receive Download Link

Attachmore is unique in that it provides the download link for you to send and share while the upload is occurring. No need to wait until the upload completes before getting a link to share with others. You can share the link knowing that the upload will complete.

4

Recipients Download

Recipients click the link you sent them and are taken to a download page to retrieve your files. They don't need to be member or install software. They click the download button to start the download.
Sending and sharing data is a personal between your and your recipients. The Attachmore download can be personalized or branded. Better yet. it you have webiiste integrate Attachmore. This way recipients download files from your website.
