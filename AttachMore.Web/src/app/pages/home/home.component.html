How it works

Attachmore makes it simple to send and share files that are too large to email. Avoid the pitfalls of email systems and the complexity of online storage solutions with a service specifically designed for file delivery.

Select and Upload

Files can be selected to upload using the Attachmore website or the Right Click Upload software.

Receive Download Link

A Download Link is generated which can be set to expire after a number of days or downloads.

Share Download Link

Recipients who recieve the link, simply click and are taken to a webpage to download the files.

Why Attachmore

We believe online storage solutions like Dropbox or Google Drive are great for backup, archiving and collaborating, but are too cumbersome for sending and sharing files especially for business purposes.

Attachmore is a better way to send and share files. It works the way you work by enhancing your current communication tools instead of replacing them.

Simple

Attachmore is just 3 steps to send large files: Select, Upload, and Share link. Recipients use links to download from a web page. They don't need an Attachmore account. Links automatically expire and files automatically purge as you set.

Secure

All uploads and downloads occur using 256 AES encyrption. Download links can be password protected or set to collect the downloaders information like email, name or company. Also links can be set to automatically expire to limit exposure.

Professional

Attachmore serves business. We value the privacy of communications between you and your recipients. For this reason we do not generate revenue by displaying third party advertisements in the Attachmore process.

Reliable

Attachmore maintains the best operating architecture built with redundancy in mind. We maintain a 99.9999% uptime rating. Rest assured knowing recipients will always have access to your files.

Integrate into your Website

The best way to send and share files is to use your website, not ours.

Integrate Attachmore Widgets into your website. Download links will use your website's address. Recipients visit your website to download. Also site visitors can upload files to you using Attachmore Upload widgets. Keep them within your brand and Attachmore behind the scenes.

Right Click Upload Software

Select and Upload Files without going to a website. No logins, No Hassles.

The Attachmore software adds extensions to the Right Click shortcut menu of your computer’s operating system. When Right Clicking on a file, a group of files or a folder you can choose "Upload to Attachmore". Selected files upload and a download link is presented and ready to share.

Team Accounts

Support your entire team with one Attachmore account. Each person on your team can be a sub-user of your account and use Attachmore to send and receive files. As the administrator you now have a permanent record of all files shared by your team with your clients.

Additional Features

Secure Links

Uploads and downloads occur using 256bit AES encyrption. Downloads links can be password protected and require downloaders to provide name, email, or company.

Auto Expire - Auto Purge

Increase security by limiting access. Download Links can expire based on days or downloads. Also purge dates can be set to automtically delete older files from storage.

Notifications and Tracking

Receive email or text messages when files are downloaded. All download activity for each link is logged and recorded in your account later review.

Long Term Archiving

Archive all files or just certain files uploaded to Attachmore. Archived files with expired download links can be reactivated to allow recipients to download again.

Guest Links

Create limited use links that others can use to upload files to you. Email or text notifications will let you know files have arrived.

Payment for Download
